How do I install a wallpaper?

In the Control Panel, click "Add New Background", the Wallpaper Explorer will open. Proceed to enter the index number for the wallpaper you want (e.g 6).

How do I uninstall a wallpaper?

Right click on the icon of your wallpaper in Control Panel, select Uninstall.
